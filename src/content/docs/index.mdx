**Solid UI is open code.**

You copy components into your app, own them, and customize them as your design system evolves.

## Why this approach

- **Full control:** the component code lives in your project.
- **Composable APIs:** components follow shared patterns.
- **Great defaults:** clean styling and accessible behavior out of the box.
- **Registry-ready:** components can be distributed and reused.

## Next steps

- Start in the [components section](/docs/components).
- Open a specific component page to see installation and usage.
- Explore and adapt the primitives to your project style.
